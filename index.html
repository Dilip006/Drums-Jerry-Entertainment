About Us

Drums Jerry Entertainment is a multidisciplinary creative house specializing in music production, film, short films, live events, and performance arts. Founded by Glady Gerad, an accomplished entrepreneur with over 18 years of industry experience, our identity is rooted in innovation, authenticity, and the celebration of Tamil culture.

Beyond content creation, we are passionate about identifying and nurturing new talents. Through our productions and events, we actively provide opportunities for emerging artists, musicians, and performers to showcase their abilities and build their careers. We believe in storytelling that uplifts voices, empowers communities, and preserves cultural heritage through modern artistic expression.
