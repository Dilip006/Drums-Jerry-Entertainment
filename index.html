About Us

Drums Jerry Entertainment is a dynamic creative powerhouse with over 18 years of industry leadership, founded by a seasoned entrepreneur. Operating across 23 cities with a team of 132 employees, we’ve empowered over 6000 professionals and mentored 1500+ students through our work in music, film, event production, and performing arts. Our mission is to blend cultural depth with modern creativity, delivering meaningful content that resonates globally.

We have produced acclaimed musical works including விவசாயி, வெல்லப்போறான் விவசாயி, ஈடற்றவர், and an upcoming 7-song album titled "உறவுகள்". In cinema, we’ve created impactful short films like உள்ளதை உள்ளபடி 1 & 2, இக்கரைக்கு அக்கரை பச்சை, and போர்நிலத்தான், with feature films கொடியோர் and கடலின் மறுபக்கம் currently in production. As event organizers, we proudly host large-scale cultural events such as வாழ்க தமிழ் and தமிழர் திருவிழா, fostering heritage and unity.

Our founder, a celebrated drummer and vocalist, has performed live on more than 50 stages over 30 years. He also plays a lead role as an actor in short films like காட்டிப்பிழை and upcoming films including கொடியோர் and கடலின் மறுபக்கம். Drums Jerry Entertainment continues to stand at the forefront of independent Tamil entertainment—where art, identity, and passion meet purpose.
