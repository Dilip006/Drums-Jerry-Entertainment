About Us

Drums Jerry Entertainment is a dynamic creative powerhouse is founded by visionary artist and entrepreneur Mr. Glady Gerad, our mission is to merge cultural richness with contemporary expression—crafting powerful content that connects deeply with audiences worldwide.

Our music portfolio features critically acclaimed works such as விவசாயி, வெல்லப்போறான் விவசாயி, ஈடற்றவர், and the upcoming 7-track album உறவுகள். In film, we’ve brought to life impactful short films including உள்ளதை உள்ளபடி 1 & 2, இக்கரைக்கு அக்கரை பச்சை, போர்நிலத்தான், and are currently producing full-length features கொடியோர் and கடலின் மறுபக்கம். As proud cultural ambassadors, we organize major Tamil heritage events like வாழ்க தமிழ் and தமிழர் திருவிழா, celebrating identity and unity through community. We are also passionate about discovering and nurturing new talent—giving aspiring musicians, actors, and creators the platform and opportunity to shine. Our production house serves as a launchpad for fresh voices and emerging artists across the Tamil creative landscape.

Our founder—a celebrated drummer, singer, and actor—has captivated audiences in over 50+ live performances across three decades. His on-screen presence also shines in short films like காட்டிப்பிழை and leading roles in the upcoming titles கொடியோர் and கடலின் மறுபக்கம். At Drums Jerry Entertainment, we continue to push the boundaries of independent Tamil media—where art meets purpose, and every project echoes our commitment to culture, creativity, and storytelling.
